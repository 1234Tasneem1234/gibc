Green Inclusive Business Champion (Green IB Champion)

A Unique Competition!!

"Green Inclusive Business Champion" is a pioneering initiative and innovative platform for promoting green innovation and entrepreneurship in Bangladesh that also contribute in economic inclusion. Such ventures are clearly associated with 17 SDG goals and impact investments. It comprises both ‘ongoing ventures’ [small scale but adoptable or widely replicable] and ‘ideas’ that are contributing, or have great potentials to contribute. In this kind of innovative business ventures, there must be attempts to integrate poor as customers or suppliers/distributors.

Vision of the Initiative!!

Integrating ‘green inclusive business’ ventures and ideas to the national economy and international platforms for enhancing inclusive and green growth of Bangladesh.

Key Objectives!!

Recognizing ‘green-inclusive business ventures/ideas’ as part of promoting green and inclusive growth. Helping ‘green-inclusive business ventures/ideas’ to draw support of the policymakers and financing institutions. Drawing attention of the policymakers to stimulate ‘green-inclusive business ventures/ideas’ for attaining SDG goals.

Phases of the Ventures Included!!  

Starting Phase: Ideas on Business start-ups that intend to apply green-inclusive approach. Adoption Phase: Small and micro enterprises in the process of adopting green and inclusive business models. Scaling-up Phase: Operational green-inclusive business models at very small scale or localized that may be replicated and scaled-up.

What are the Eligible Businesses/Business Ideas??

The competition is open to Environmentally Sustainable Business Activities/Ventures/Business Ideas (Individual/ Group of Individuals/organization) fall in the category of Micro and Small Enterprises [Goods and/or Services] in any economic sector that contribute to the achievement of the one or more than one of the following Sustainable Development Goals/Targets/Concerns. Business Activities/Ventures/Business Ideas are expected to support the role of businesses in engaging local inhabitants, based on local resource utilization, reviving production traditions, and engaging local markets.

  • Food Security and Sustainable Agriculture
  • Good Health and Wellbeing
  • Quality Education
  • Gender Equality
  • Clean Water and Sanitation
  • Sustainable Clean Energy & Energy Efficiency

How many to be Chosen and What are the Broad Selection Criteria?

The Green Inclusive Business Champions [Business Activities/Ventures/Business Ideas] would be selected for Award based on the following Broad criteria:

  • Business Activities/Ventures/Business Ideas are Environmentally Sustainable
  • Activities/Ventures/ Ideas are Financially/Economically Viable.
  • Business Activities/Ventures/ Ideas engage low-income people at input, production or consumption phase.
  • Business Activities/Ventures must be replicable and scalable.
  • Business Ideas must be applied, replicable and scalable.
  • Business Activities/Ventures/Business Ideas should engage local resources, local people and utilize traditional skills.

Selection Flow!!

Primary Round:Short Listing of the Business Activities/Ventures/Business Ideas by the Panel of Judges.

Qualifying Round: Presentation Round by the Applicant before the Panel of Judges [Live at Online Platform]

Final Round: Short Presentation Question-Answer with the Qualified Applicants by the Panel of Judges [Live at TV Channel]
